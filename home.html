The Sport

Ultimate is a sport that combines running, jumping, and coordination, as opposing teams attempt to move the disc upfield and score in endzones by passing among teammates. Ultimate is played on a football-sized field with each team fielding seven players at a time. Just like other team sports, there is extensive strategy, training, and teamwork behind a competitive ultimate game. For more details on ultimate, check out Ultimate in 10 Simple Rules, or take the time to read through the official rules.
